How to Use Fire Spread Simulation
1. Select Location

Use the search box or enter coordinates to select an area on the world map. Click "Select Area" to extract terrain data.

2. Start Fire

Once terrain is loaded, click anywhere on the map to ignite a fire at that location.

3. Control Simulation

Use the simulation controls to start, pause, or step through the fire spread simulation.

4. Adjust Weather

Modify wind speed, direction, humidity, and temperature to see how weather affects fire behavior.

Color Legend
  • Green - Forest and vegetation (fuel)
  • Red - Active fire
  • Dark Gray - Burned areas
  • Blue - Water bodies
  • Gray - Urban areas
